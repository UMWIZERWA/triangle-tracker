TRIANGLE-TRACKER

Triangle is a three-sided polygon. This closed figure consists of three lines which are linked at the endpoint to each other. The main properties of the triangle are that the sum of the interior angle of the triangle will be 180° and the sum of the exterior angles will be 360° always. Triangle can be classified according to their sides and interior angles.
